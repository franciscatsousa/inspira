Applications for mentoring cicle

Registrations for the mentoring cycle are now open!

Each cycle lasts one academic year and includes individual mentoring sessions (one-to-one) between mentor and mentee, which must be scheduled between the two depending on the availability of both parties.

We collect the profiles of the mentors and mentees and match them up based on the mentor's skills and the areas in which the mentees are looking for the most support.
