Job market and recruitment processes

Join us for a round table discussion on the job market and recruitment processes, where you can hear the perspectives of professionals from the fields of Human Resources and Informatics!

You will then have the opportunity to take part in the practical workshop “Applying to a tech startup: what to do before, during and after the interview”, where you will be able to develop your skills in recruitment processes.

All activities will be conducted in English!
