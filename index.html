What can inspira offer you?

INSPIRA is a mentoring program at the Department of Informatics Engineering of the University of Coimbra and its goal is to attract and retain female talent in informatics.

INSPIRA offers mentorship and exposure to role models. By engaging with INSPIRA, you’ll meet more experience individuals who have walked similar paths and are eager to share their experience and guidance with you.

Additionally, you'll have the opportunity to broaden your network, connecting with people from diverse backgrounds, fostering collaboration and support.

Join our inspiring events, from career mapping in ICT to resilience building, tailored to empower you to thrive both your career and personal life.

Who is INSPIRA for and why is it needed?

In today's tech-driven world, women's participation in informatics extends beyond gender; it's a societal priority. With every job now involving technology, closing the gender gap not only broadens the talent pool but also stimulates digital economic growth. Moreover, diverse perspectives are key to fostering creativity and innovation.

Yes, our priority is to support women in tech, but lasting change requires everyone's participation. So, everyone is welcome to take part! EVERYONE is welcome to join our events throughout the year and contribute to our collective effort towards fostering an inclusive environment!

Join us in our path towards diversity, equality, and equity!
