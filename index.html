Who is it for
and why did
it start?

In today's technology-driven world, the active involvement of women in informatics is not just a matter of gender, but a concern for society as a whole. Embracing diverse perspectives fosters creativity and innovation, crucial as technology becomes increasingly intertwined with our lives.

Closing the gender gap in this field, not only taps into a broader talent pool but also drives the digital economy at a European level. Challenging stereotypes in this field promotes mutual support and fosters an inclusive environment, advancing equality and equity.
